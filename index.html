What is Internaly?

When new nternational students travel oversees to pursue their education, they need proper guidance and accurate information to shape their journey. However, there wasn't a platform to achieve this in an organized manner. Well, until now...

Internaly is a new platform where current international students can connect with international students who are alumni. The platform focuses on creating a village all around the world where international students can seek advice specifically from other international student alumni and advisors.

Share your academic, travelling, and cultural experiences with international students everywhere!

Connect with International Student Alumni and ask questions, practice interviews, and get invaluable tips!

Talk to International Student Advisors to learn about specific schools can help you!
